## Does K-CNAS exceed the limits of Kubernetes?

K-CNAS is a Kubernetes operator that extends the capabilities of Kubernetes to support the creation and management of web applications. It is built on top of Kubernetes and provides a declarative way to define and manage web applications. Thus it results in many custom resources and complex interactions between them which some may consider to be a violation of the limits of Kubernetes.
